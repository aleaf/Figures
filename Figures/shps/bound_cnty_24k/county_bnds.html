Description:
Abstract:
This polygon shapefile represents boundaries of the 72 counties in Wisconsin. The data is derived from 1:24,000-scale sources.
Purpose:
This data set provides a generalized outline of the 72 counties in Wisconsin. It is not intended for use as a representation of statutory county boundaries.

The level of accuracy does not support detailed local analysis. The data are intended for use with ArcView, ArcInfo, or other GIS software which support shapefile format data.

Process_Step:
Process_Description:
Linework from the Wisconsin 24K State Boundaries data layer was used for portions of county boundaries that form the periphery of the state. Linework from the 1:24,000-scale Landnet layer were used for portions of county boundaries that coincide with PLSS lines. Portions of county boundaries in surface water features in the interior of the state were digitized on-screen using 1:24,000-scale digital raster graphics. Linework from the 1:24,000-scale Hydrography layer were used for the portion of the shoreline around Sturgeon Bay in Door County.

In all cases, the US Geological Survey's 1:24,000-scale digital raster graphics were used as the authority for the location of county boundary lines.
